
## Selection of Best Pre-trained Model for Text Generation using TOPSIS

### Domain
News Article Text Generation

### Models
GPT-2, GPT-Neo, T5, BART-Large, XLNet

### Parameters
Perplexity, BLEU, ROUGE, Inference Time, Model Size, Human Evaluation Score

### Method
TOPSIS multi-criteria decision making

### Conclusion
BART-Large is the optimal model based on TOPSIS ranking.
